Accomplished:

  • We made progress on some of the "meatier" stories.
  • We worked on our communication because the previous approach (Slack) was not working.
  • We revised our backlog by both adding an item that was missed and sanity-checking our effort estimates.

Challenges:
  • Our scope crept up by 52 units. We are concerned that this may keep happening, and as a result we may need to go even faster than we thought to be in good shape by the end.
  • On a psychological note, although we increased our velocity from 74 to 88, which we thought we'd feel good about, the scope creep was a buzzkill.
  • Waffle turned out to be a bit of a pain because it does not show any stories from more than 7 days ago. We are currently using excel as the primary tracker as a result.
  • Our current trajectory implies we will not finish in time, with an even bigger gap than last time.
  • It was discovered very late in this Sprint and just prior to this report being done that some of our "Done" tasks were actually not passing the mocha tests.
Roadblocks:
  • None so far!

Before the next report we hope to:

  • Increase our velocity even further.
  • Figure out how much of a buffer we need to build in to actually have finished the task by the end.
  • Review all of the Done tasks to make sure they are passing their respective mocha tests.
